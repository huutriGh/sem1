Trung Nguyen Coffee

Trung nguyen Coffee is most famous for its strong taste and aroma. It is the leading local brand in Viet Nam and a big explorter - you can find Trung Nguyen coffee in over 70 countries in the world. There are wide range of products diverse in the mix of coffee beans and processing method

Vinacafe

Focusing more on the mild side, Vinacafe is among the best choice for instant blended coffee. This brand is most famous for its creamy, hazelnutty favor for those with a sweet tooth. Vinacafe has been the coffee of our childhood and still common among Vietnamese household.The most popular Vinacafe 3-in-1, Wake up cafe and Weasel favor.

Nescafe

NESCAFE - the famous worldwide brand, comes with a lot of choices for discerning market of VietNamese Coffee connoisseurs.Among their products, instant one are top of mind. Their blended coffee comes in different levels of strong taste that can be no less delicious than their ground coffee counterparts. Their NESCAFE cans Mocha/Latte are a good choice when you just want a pack of ready-made goodness.
